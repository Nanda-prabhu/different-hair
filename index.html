About us

We manufacturers of hair extensions do not just want to sell products, but we aim to provide solutions for the customer in this field. We develop a system for customers to buy their dreads from us. It was possible to provide solutions in the hair industry through our founder Isaac Bracha 's valuable research on hair. And the research never stops here. Our data team (2nd division) continues our research to find the customer problems and needs. Also our marketing team(3rd division) works to make customers' dreams come true.

Our story

Transforming communities. Each individual Different Hair Product is handmade with the utmost precision and care by the expert craftswomen of the Tribes - "the people of the Different Hair" Across different weaving communities, we are working to create jobs for mothers and build a foundation for positive community transformation.
